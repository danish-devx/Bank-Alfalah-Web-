Bank Alfalah Islamic Banking was established as a dedicated Islamic banking
division within Bank Alfalah Limited in 2003 to offer its customers products and
services that are Shari’ah compliant. The Bank has around 453 dedicated
branches across 106 cities along with an Islamic banking app providing to
customers’ with Shari’ah compliant banking solutions. Bank Alfalah Islamic
Banking comprises a team of professionals who design and structure Islamic
financial solutions and ensure that they are in line with Shari’ah principles on
Islamic banking and finance. These solutions are developed under the guidance
of a dedicated Shari’ah Board.
